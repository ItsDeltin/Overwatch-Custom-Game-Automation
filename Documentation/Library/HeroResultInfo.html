Deltin.CustomGameAutomation.HeroResultInfo Enumerator
Result info of GetHero().
ValueDescription
Success The hero the player was playing was successfully found.
PlayerWasDead Can't get the hero the player was playing because the player is dead. Try rescanning when the player is alive again.
NoHeroChosen The player did not choose a hero.
SlotEmpty The slot was empty.
NoCompatibleHeroFound Could not tell what hero the player is playing. Chances are if you get this it is a bug with GetHero().
